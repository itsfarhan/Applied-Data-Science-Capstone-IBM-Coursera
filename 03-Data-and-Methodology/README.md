## 🧩 Business Problem

> Identifying the optimal location for a new **Indian restaurant in NYC** by analyzing market saturation and venue density. This section defines the strategic framework used for the recommendation.

**Core Objectives:**
- Analyze competition density across all 5 NYC boroughs.
- Identify "Blue Ocean" opportunities (high demand, low competition).
- Standardize the decision-making criteria for stakeholders.
