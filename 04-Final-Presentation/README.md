## 💡 Strategic Insight

Through extensive geospatial clustering and saturation analysis, we discovered that while Manhattan is the most "popular" for dining, **Staten Island** offers the most significant growth potential due to its extremely low saturation of Indian cuisine relative to its growing resident population.
